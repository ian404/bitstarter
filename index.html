I'm a fine artist then a home baker and I love styling sweet treats and party tables. I do parties for all ages, baby showers, engagements, weddings, book club days. I bake novelty cakes, cake pops, sugar cookies and other treats with unusual flavours and twists. Flavours, texture and ultimate presentation are an absolute priority for me when filling an order, so you're getting the freshest ingredients with maximum style. I bridge the gap between standard party cake ideas and deli/patisserie treats with my own flamboyance and pursuit of the perfect taste treat. Your party is the place where you can express what you love through colours, fabrics, fashion, themes, your favourite foods, plants, momentoes and nic nacs.

Make an appointment for a consultation. Together we'll explore ideas and create a stunning visual feast that will have you and your guests talking for ages after the event. A picture says more than a thousand words and if it's worth doing, it's worth doing utterly beautifully.
